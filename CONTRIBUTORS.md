# Contributors

This repository recognizes code, documentation, testing, design, release, and
community contributions after they are public and attributable.

## Beta tester recognition

Beta testers may opt into public recognition after submitting actionable
feedback through the `beta-feedback` GitHub Discussions category or through a
linked Linear issue labeled `source:beta`.

Recognition entries use only public names or handles. Do not list email
addresses, private company names, customer project names, board files,
schematics, BOMs, secrets, or non-public design details.
